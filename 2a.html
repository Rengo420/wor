    MAKHLOUF SONS

    Flexible Packaging Experts

    We are Makhlouf Sons Co, a packaging and packing company who have been established for more than 60 years. We provide services for importing and exporting as well as the local sectors for companies working in various fields. Such as retail, pharmaceutical, food industry companies and companies exporting vegetables and fruits. We are able produce various products such as ziplock bags, cling film, stretch film, trash bags, aluminium foil , tape, nappy / sanitary towel bags, freezer bags and many more home utility bags. Our mission moving forward is to provide our customers with quality products that are cost efficient but 100% reusable and could be repurposed starting from our packaging to our philosophy.
